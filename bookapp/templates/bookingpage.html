Green Fees

At Burch's Golf Course, we offer a variety of green fee options to suit every golfer, whether you’re here for a quick round or a full day of play. Our fees vary depending on the time of day and week:

  • Weekday costs: 9 Holes: £25 per person 18 Holes: £45 per person
  • Weekend costs: 9 Holes: £35 per person 18 Holes: £65 per person
  • Evening costs: Play until sunset: £25

Golf Buggies

  • Buggies are available to rent upon arrival at the clubhouse. Our fleet of buggies ensures a comfortable and enjoyable experience on the course, allowing you to focus on your game while easily navigating between holes.
  • Buggies work on a first come first serve basis and booking is not available currently.
  • There are 6 buggies available costing £25 per round ontop of the green fee.

Practise Area

Warm up and perfect your swing before hitting the course! At Burch's Golf Club, we’re pleased to offer a dedicated practice area. Our practice facilities include:

  • Driving Range: Fine-tune your long shots with ample space to practice drives and full swings.
  • Putting Green: Get a feel for the greens with our putting area, designed to mimic course conditions.
  • Chipping Area: Perfect your short game with dedicated space for practicing chips and bunker shots.
